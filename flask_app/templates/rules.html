Hello, and welcome to Ultimate Tic Tac Toe.

There are 5 rules and a few more notes: 1. The '*' symbol denotes a place you can play 2. You win a small board by getting three of your pieces in a row 3. You win the game by getting three in a row on the big board 4. Wherever you play on one of the smaller tic tac toe boards, your opponent gets to place a piece in the corresponding larger board (this is called 'sending' a player to their next board) 5. If you send your opponent to a smaller board that is already won, they can play anywhere on the big board that is not full Note: the last move your opponent played will be highlighted in red Note: the 3x3 square at the bottom of the screen is the state of the big board
